Shane's Rice Bowl

This is a dish that I myself have created and had the pleasure to perfect over time.
It is simple but a great breakfast that anyone can enjoy from the prescence of their own home. I hope that everyone is able to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Butter
  • Spices
    • Protein
    • Chicken or Fish works
  • Food Stock: chx/beef/fish
  • Lemon juice
  • Eggs(optional)

Steps

    1. Prepare Rice:
    1. Poor rice into a bowl and cover with water;allow rice to sit for 30 mins
    2. After 30 mins, drain water from rice and por rice into pot
    3. Fill pot with enough water that it touches the first finger knuckle if finger is placed on rice
    4. Boil on med-hi for 10 minutes, after 10 move to med-lo for 5 minutes and finally off for 5 minutes
    5. Place rice into a bowl and refrigerate as long as possible
  2. Prepare pan buy adding butter, spices and stock
  3. Place pan on med-hi and one dimmering place protein
  4. Allow 5 min and add lemon juice and wait for fish to finish
  5. Lastly prepare the eggs how ever you would like(I always suggest sunny side :)
  6. Finally mix all and enjoy
