How to make Spaghetti

Today we will be learning how to make spaghetti. Spaghetti is an
Italian dish consisting of noodles, tomato sauce and ground protein.
Sometimes I even add some parmesean cheese. Please enjoy!!

Ingredients

  1. Ground Beef
  2. Tomato Sauce
  3. Parmesean Cheese
  4. Spaghetti Noodles
  5. Onion Powder
  6. Garlic Powder
  7. Salt

Steps

    • Prepare the Meat:
    • In a bowl place ground beef/turkey/chicken and mix with salt, onion and garlic powder
    • Place ground beef/turkey/chicken in pan on med-hi
    • Allow to cook until meat is finished
  • Add tomato sauce to cooked meat and place on low to simmer
  • Add sugar(optional)
  • Place noodles into a pot of boiling water until soft
  • Plate noodles and add meat sauce on top
  • Garnish with evenly spread parmesean as a top layer
