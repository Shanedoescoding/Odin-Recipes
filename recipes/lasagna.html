Lasagna

This is an italian dish called lasagna

This dish is a layered pasta generally consisting of 2-3 types of cheese, ground beef, and the pasta noodles. It is
traditionally italian but it has been made in different variations for decades.
Enjoy this recipe and enjoy the taste too.

Ingredients

    • Cheese:
    • RICOTTA
    • Parmesean
    • Mozzerella
    • Meats:
    • Italian Sausage
    • Ground Beef
  • Spices
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Lasagna

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water
    2. Prepare Meat Sauce:
    1. Cook sausage and beef together
    2. Add onion and garlic
    3. Drain extra liquids
    4. Mix in pasta sauce and allow time to simmer and thicken
    3. Create cheese mixture
    1. Add all cheeses together in one bowl
    2. Mix all seasonings into bowl with cheeses
    4. Layer & Bake
    1. Layer meats
    2. Layer noodles
    3. Layer cheese
    4. Repeat and finish with a top layer of noodles and sauce
    5. Bake for 1 hour then remove foild and bake for 15 minutes
